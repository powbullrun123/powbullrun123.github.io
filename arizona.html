About Us

What Is Surplus Fund Recovery?

When a property is foreclosed or sold at a tax auction for more than what was owed, the extra money — called surplus funds — is held by the county or state. Most people never know this money exists.

At Rightful Returns Recovery, we specialize in tracking down these funds across all of Arizona — from Maricopa to Mohave County — and reuniting them with their rightful owners.

There is no cost to you upfront. We work on contingency — we only earn a fee if we successfully recover money for you.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are surplus funds legitimate?

Yes. Arizona law allows former property owners to claim excess proceeds after foreclosure or tax sales. These are real funds held by the county court waiting to be claimed.

How long does the process take?

Timelines vary by county but typically range from several weeks to a few months. We keep you updated throughout the entire process.

What if no funds are found?

There is no obligation and no fee. Our search is completely free and risk-free for you.

Which Arizona counties do you cover?

We cover all Arizona counties including Maricopa, Pima, Pinal, Yavapai, Mohave, Yuma, Coconino, and more.
