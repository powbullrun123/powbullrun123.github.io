To claim surplus funds, you generally need to provide documentation such as proof of prior ownership, government‑issued photo ID, foreclosure case information, and possibly a claim form filed with the county or court that holds the funds. :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}

The specific process varies by jurisdiction and type of sale, but many courts or trustees will require notarized affidavits and proof of entitlement before releasing funds. :contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}
