Who We Are

Built on Integrity.
Driven by Purpose.

Rightful Returns Recovery was founded on a simple but powerful belief: people deserve what's rightfully theirs. Every year, thousands of homeowners lose properties to foreclosure or tax sale — and never find out the county is holding surplus funds in their name.

We built this company to close that gap. Our team researches county court records, tax sale databases, and public records across all 50 states to locate unclaimed surplus funds and excess proceeds — then we do all the work to get that money back to you.

We work entirely on contingency. Zero upfront cost. If we don't recover money for you, you owe us nothing. That's not a sales line — that's our promise.
