Privacy Policy & Data Security

Rightful Returns Recovery uses your mobile number exclusively for communications regarding your surplus fund recovery case. We do not sell or share SMS opt-in data with third parties for marketing purposes. For our complete Privacy Policy, including data collection, use, and your rights, see our full Privacy Policy page.

TCPA Consent

By providing your phone number, you authorize Rightful Returns Recovery to contact you by telephone and text message, including by automated systems, regarding your inquiry. Consent to receive calls or texts is not a condition of any purchase or service. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to a text, or by calling 1 (800) 428-2022.

CAN-SPAM Policy

If you provide your email address, we will only send communications that comply with the CAN-SPAM Act, including a valid sender identity, accurate subject lines, our physical business address, and a working unsubscribe method in every marketing email. See our full CAN-SPAM disclosure.
