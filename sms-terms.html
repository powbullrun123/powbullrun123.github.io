Recovery confirmations — Notifications when funds have been located or recovered
General support — Responses to your inquiries about your case

How to Opt In — Consent to Receive Text Messages

You may provide your consent to receive text messages from Rightful Returns Recovery through any of the following methods:

Method 1 — Keyword Reply (Text-Back Opt-In):
When you receive an initial outreach message from Rightful Returns Recovery, reply with any of the following keywords to confirm your consent:

YES I AGREE AGREE JOIN START

Upon receipt of your opt-in keyword, you will immediately receive a confirmation message with program details, message frequency, opt-out instructions, and contact information. Your consent is recorded at the date and time of your reply.

Method 2 — Web Form:
By completing the contact or opt-in form at www.rightfulreturnsrecovery.com and checking the SMS consent checkbox, you expressly consent to receive text messages at the mobile number provided.

Method 3 — Written or Signed Agreement:
By signing a paper SMS Consent & Authorization Form provided by Rightful Returns Recovery in person or by mail.

Important: Your consent is obtained only through one of the methods above. We will never add you to our messaging list without your clear, affirmative consent. Consent records are retained for a minimum of 4 years.
