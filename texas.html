Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have to claim surplus funds in Texas?

Texas Tax Code provides a 2-year window from the date of the tax sale for former owners to claim excess proceeds. It's critical to act quickly before the deadline passes.

Which Texas counties do you cover?

All 254 Texas counties, including Harris (Houston), Dallas, Bexar (San Antonio), Tarrant (Fort Worth), Travis (Austin), Collin, Denton, El Paso, and more.

What types of sales generate surplus funds in Texas?

Both tax sales (conducted by county tax assessors) and foreclosure sales can generate surplus funds when the property sells for more than what is owed.

What if no funds are found?

There is no obligation and no fee. Our search is completely free and risk-free for you.
